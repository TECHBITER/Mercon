Trackon Couriers
Courier Service

Trackon is a step ahead ... and always.

Today its vast network spread across length and breadth of India has made it a strong player handling close to 1,80,000 shipments per day within its network. With presence in more than 1400 locations, it has strengthened its short and long route surface operations which are growing by the day apart from moving shipments by air-mode to all major connected cities. In an endeavour to innovate and improve our services; we launched our super express service "Prime Track"- in 2010 on select routes. Phenomenal success of new product in short period speaks volumes about our organisational strength.

Reachon FastBuz
Point to Point Courier

A joint venture of Trackon couriers & Kerala SRTC

Reachon Fastbuz is a joint venture of Trackon Couriers Pvt. Ltd. and the Kerala State Road Transport Corporation. Fastbuz service uses the Kerala State Road Transport Corporation passenger transport vehicles to carry packages across the state and major cities of neighbouring states, which are further managed and delivered through Depot Business Agencies in bus stations, Franchisees in major towns and Collection Centres spread across the state.
